Homework vs Assignment

Homework is usually work that is assigned by a teacher to be completed outside of class time, often as a way of reinforcing and practicing concepts that were taught in class. Homework can be daily or weekly, and it can take the form of exercises, problem sets, readings, or research projects. Homework is usually graded and counts towards a student's overall grade in the course.

Assignments, on the other hand, are broader in scope and can take various forms, such as essays, research papers, presentations, or creative projects. Assignments are typically more complex and require a higher level of research, critical thinking, and analysis than homework. Assignments are often longer-term projects that are assigned over the course of a semester or a term, and they often require students to demonstrate mastery of specific skills or concepts.
